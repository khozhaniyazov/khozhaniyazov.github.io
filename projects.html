Project 1: Predictive Maintenance using Machine Learning

This project aimed to predict equipment failures in an industrial setting using machine learning techniques. By analyzing historical maintenance data and sensor readings, we developed a predictive model to identify potential issues before they occur, thus optimizing maintenance schedules and reducing downtime.

Project 2: Sentiment Analysis of Customer Reviews

In this project, we analyzed customer reviews from an e-commerce platform to understand customer sentiment towards different products. By employing natural language processing techniques, we categorized reviews into positive, negative, or neutral sentiments, providing valuable insights for product improvement and marketing strategies.
